Maze Escape!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys (desktop) or on-screen buttons (mobile) to move the green player.
  • Navigate through the maze without touching the black walls.
  • Collect bonus stars for extra points.
  • Reach the gold exit to advance to the next level.
  • Try to finish as fast as possible and collect as many bonuses as you can!
